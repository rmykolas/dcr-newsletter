Decred Project Origin
As Decred takes steps towards operating as a Decentralized Autonomous Entity, the Politeia Proposal System plays a vital role, endowing the stakeholders with sovereignty to manage the spending of Decred’s Treasury and Decred’s policy. Proposals to date include new software features, marketing expenditures, partner integrations, contractor management, bug bounty, and research. Politeia is a governance platform that uses dcrtime to cryptographically verify identities and record all activity, managing the submission, discussion and voting of proposals. Proposals are submitted for a small fee, reviewed for spam and completeness, then published for discussion by the community. This process allows proposals to be iterated and refined before the owner authorizes voting to begin.
When voting is started, a snap picture of the ticket pool is taken to determine voting eligibility, and ticket holders have roughly one week to vote. Once a proposal passes, the owner is authorized to begin work, knowing their efforts are supported by the community and financed by the Treasury.
